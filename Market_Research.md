# MarketResearch

## 1. Market & Trends Overview  

The game Dishaster is strategically positioned at the confluence of several high-traffic and popular genres: Time Management/Simulation, Casual/Mid-Core Games, and Comedic Stealth. The primary target platforms are Mobile (iOS/Android) and PC (Steam).

### Key Market Data & Trends:
Mobile Gaming Market (Global):

### Scale:
The mobile segment dominates the gaming industry, boasting annual global revenues exceeding [US$126.06bn in 2025 -Statista].

### Genre Status:
Time Management, Cooking, and Casual Simulation titles enjoy immense popularity on mobile, typically utilizing the Free-to-Play (F2P) model.

### Current Trend: 
There is a significant focus on integrating "Meta-Game" elements (e.g., character customization, long-term story arcs, permanent upgrades/decorating the staff room) into casual games. This strategy is essential for increasing player retention (LTV) and extending the product's long-term lifecycle beyond the core gameplay loop.

### PC Casual & Co-op Chaos Market (Steam):

Trend: Platforms like Steam exhibit sustained and growing interest in "Co-op Chaos" titles (Overcooked!, PlateUp!) and unique, humorous, physics-based simulations. This audience is willing to pay a Premium upfront for high-quality, non-F2P experiences.

Opportunity: Dishaster is positioned to capitalize on the desire for chaotic gameplay but with a unique solo-player focus. It translates the high-pressure, comedic energy of competitive co-op into a personal, narrative-driven experience centered on social stealth and human absurdity.

### The Israeli Market Context:

The Israeli audience is a rapid adopter of global mobile gaming trends. Should the game incorporate local cultural humor or settings (e.g., specific event types or recognizable character archetypes), it could generate local virality and foster a strong, immediate connection with the domestic audience.
